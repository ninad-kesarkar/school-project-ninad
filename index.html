Yashwantrao Bhonsale Polytechnic

Yashwantrao Bhonsale Polytechnic (YBP) is the first ever Polytechnic College in Sindhudurrg district introducing pinnacle of excellence in engineering education. The commitment for total quality management (TQM) and discipline is prime slogan and motto of our college.

About Us

Yashwantrao Bhonsale Polytechnic, Sawantwadi, established in 2014, is a comprehensive Technical Institute located at Charathe (Vazarwadi), Sawnatwadi, enjoying a picturesque setting that is unique among the educational set-up of this region of Maharashtra. Having a distance of about 2.5 km. from city of Sawantwadi, it is a quiet and peaceful place maintaining an atmosphere where faculty and students would take the process of teaching and learning seriously
